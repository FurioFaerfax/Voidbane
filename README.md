## Update 0.3.0 added 3 Biomes accessed via Fragment keys!

***

Voidbane is a Mod that adds a couple of Items, blocks, Npcs and features. The most important thing is a story, driven by my interpretation how the Cursebreaker Story could eventually start.

## Currently featured Content:

*   Shadowlab: The main Workstation of the ModCurrently it uses just placeholder textures, because I not figured out a design for it yet texture wise)
*   Duskmoon: A new flower
*   Dawnberries: A new Berry Bush
*   Twilight Beans: The first product of the shadowlab, a combination of Dusk and Dawn
*   Twilight Essence: An Essence squeezed out of the Twilight beans, used mainly for the Infusement mechanic
*   Twilight Prism: an addon for the Shadowlab
*   Dragon Scales
*   2 Flasks with liquid, progression related
*   3 Portal Keys to enter Biome Instances for Mod progression
*   Infused Bow and 2 Arrows(Currently I am just adding the progression related Infusements)
*   3 Biomes  
    *   Hermits Grove: a Sky Island Biome where the Hermits Hut will generate
    *   Frozen Wastes: A harsh cold Environment where a structure for the Ice Dragon will spawn
    *   Darkened Caverns: A Devestated land where the Boss fight will take place

+++ **_Note to the Biomes: I added them via WorldgenV2 but I have to figure out how to just spawn 1 of the Structures or how to get a single Structure Instancew like the forgotten temple. In worst case I have a workarround in mind. However next step is to add the Interaction Mechanic to the NPCs and designing the Boss fight, and a bit coding to track the progess: Build now polish later :D_** +++
