Voidbane Instructions:
1. For starting with the Progression, just start with crafting a fragment key of the HermitsGrove
2. When you entered this instance, search for a hut, its not huge, so the Map may help.
3. Talk to the NPC, (In the current version its not working sadly, but theres a statue nearby that you can Interact with)
4. The Hermit will tell you a part of the story, after the first chapter youll be prompted to Craft the Shadow Lab at the Workbench
5. With the Shadow Lab you can make Twilight Bean seeds and the crops can then be drained to gain Twilight essence(A ingredient you need for Progression and later when I Update the Mod as well as for Infusements(Like enchanting))
6. After the next time you talk to the hermit youll get the twilight prism, its needed to upgrade the Shadowlab
7. After the 3rd Time you talked to the hermit youll be prompted to craft the Fragment Key of the frozen Wastes to talk to the dragon. This Structure is more visible to the eye even when just Exploring.
8. In this chapter(4) you need to talk to both, the hermit and the Dragon(for both its a statue in this version).
9. The Dragon will give you 2 Flasks, one of it is  needed for the last Portal Key, to the Darkened Caverns. Where the Boss fight will take place.
10. The Darkened Caverns bears a Structure Underground, the entrance is distinguishable from the rest of the Biome but the Map will help to find it better.
11. After the Fight youll need to switch to the Loot in active hand, it triggers a command for the outcome of the fight(Currently its just 1 supported)
12. Then You can talk to both NPCs again, and get the final Reward, a statue of The Void Contained Symbol
